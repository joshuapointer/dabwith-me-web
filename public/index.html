how a sesh goes

three taps. a calm minute. a perfect dab.

no apps to learn. no settings to bury. dabwith.me asks two questions and then walks the rest of the curve with you — quietly, in real time.

01 · CHOOSE tell bub what you've got

pick from 14 banger types and 24 concentrates. flat top, slurper, rosin, sauce, distillate. real shapes, real dab-temp pockets — sourced from hashwriter and terpometer data.

02 · HEAT torch slow, watch bub bloom

a realistic heat curve runs the clock based on your banger's documented warm-up time. bub's halo turns peach, then ember, then a focused red as the surface approaches your target.

03 · LIFT dab when bub turns green

cool curve uses newton's law from your banger's documented cooldown. bub hits a calm mint green exactly inside your concentrate's terpene window. no chasing, no second-guessing.

honest preview

no thermometer, no bluetooth — just an honest curve.

this is a simulation, not telemetry. dabwith.me runs newton's law of cooling using your banger's documented heat and cool times — the same numbers hashwriter and terpometer measured.

on the day you don't have a thermometer or you forgot the timer in another room, bub's curve gets you within ~10°f of the right pocket. on the day you have an IR thermometer? still use it. the app makes the wait structured, not blind.
